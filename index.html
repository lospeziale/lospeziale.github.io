Spicy, irreverent, home-grown.

“Lo Speziale” is a project born in 2018 from the chili pepper’s passion: Lorenzo and Andrea make it real. Always fascinated by nature that surround us but always working in other fiels, two years ago we muster the courage and decided to start planting chili peppers to get the most from multitude of its resources. We farm around a thousand plants of different varieties in a field in Castelbellino, in Ancona province (central Italy).
